Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking a table during peak hours (evenings and weekends) to ensure availability.

Can I bring my own games?

Absolutely! We love seeing new games. Just make sure they're complete and in good condition.

Do you host private events?

Yes! We offer private event packages for birthdays, corporate events, and special occasions. Contact us for details.

Is there parking available?

We have a dedicated parking lot behind the building with 50 spaces, plus street parking available on Meeple Street.
