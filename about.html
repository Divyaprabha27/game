Our Story

From a small collection in a garage to the city's premier gaming destination.

How We Started

Meeple Manor began in 2024 with a simple mission: to create a space where people could disconnect from their screens and reconnect with each other. Too often, we found ourselves scrolling through phones even when hanging out with friends. We noticed that board games were the perfect antidote.

What started as a pop-up event in a local library quickly grew into a passionate community. We realized that people weren't just looking for games; they were looking for belonging.

Our Values

01. Community First

We believe in building connections. Our tables are designed for groups to mingle, and our events are open to everyone.

02. Inclusivity

Gaming is for everyone. We maintain a safe, welcoming space where respect is the only rule of the house.

03. Unplugged Fun

In a digital world, we champion the tactile joy of moving pieces, rolling dice, and looking people in the eye.
